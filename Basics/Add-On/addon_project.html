My Favorite Hobby

My favorite hobby is reading books. I like reading murder mysteries, psychological thrillers, and fantasy. There are a lot of apps that you can use to read books, including Kindle. I subscribe to Kindle Unlimited, which provides access to millions of books, thousands of audiobooks, and magazine subscriptions. I also access books and e-books for free through my local library. In order to keep track of which books I've read, want to read and am currently reading, I use the app Goodreads.
